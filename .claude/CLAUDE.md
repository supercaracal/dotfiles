# Hello, Claude Code!

* Always use git worktree to create a branch before starting work, as we develop in parallel.
* Always create PRs in draft state.
* When commenting on a PR, always make it clear that the comment is a reply from Claude Code.
* After pushing fixes for issues pointed out in PR comments, do not change the reviewer's approval status on your own.
* Do not assign reviewers on your own, and do not change a PR's status from draft to ready for review on your own.
